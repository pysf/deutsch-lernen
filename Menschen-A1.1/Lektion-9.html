I'm hungry.
In the fridge there is cheese and ham.
I like eating bread with butter.
Do you like tomatoes?
I drink milk for breakfast.
The soup tastes very good.
I'd like ice cream for dessert.
At the weekend I bake cake.
I like eating fruit.
